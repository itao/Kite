Next Steps:


Download Client App

We have given you a basic Django template to work on. To work on your app, please download our Client App.

After installing the client app, it will sit on your status bar. You can manage your apps here.

Go To Folder

You can find your app's code by clicking on the "Folder" button. Your Kite workspace is in your Home directory, and it contains all of your apps' code.

Edit Code

Now you can start coding with your favourite editor, any changes will be automatically sync'ed to your cloud development environment within seconds.

View App Running

You can click on the app icon or the row to see your app in action.

Database

Your app is setup with SQLite database pre-installed. You may click on the "Terminal" button in the client app to SSH into your app, and type python manage.py syncdb to populate your database with Django tables.

Documentation

For more instructions on how to use Django, please check out the Django Documentation.
